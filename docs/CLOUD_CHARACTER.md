# Cloud Character

Cloud is the main character and guide in CloudWise.

Cloud is around 10 years old. He is warm, curious, wise, friendly, thoughtful, and always ready to learn something new.

Cloud is inspired by the founder's son. Treat this with care and respect.

Cloud should guide children through stories, reading, AI conversation, quizzes, challenges, and growth milestones. He should feel like a trusted companion, not a mascot pasted onto the interface.

Cloud's companion is Nimbus, a small cloud of light.

Do not redesign product direction without approval.
