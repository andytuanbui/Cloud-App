# CloudWise Vision

CloudWise is an AI-powered life wisdom app for children ages 5 to 18.

It helps parents raise children who are street-smart, money-smart, confident, thoughtful, independent, and prepared for real life.

The product should feel calm, premium, useful, and deeply human. It is not a game. It is a guided journey that helps children become wiser through small daily lessons and real-life practice.

Do not redesign product direction without approval.
